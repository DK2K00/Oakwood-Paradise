History



The Oakwood Paradise Company was founded in Pondicherry in 1909 during the British era by Sir Eugene Ludacrew. It is located in the famous White Town. It was started as a small furniture store with a working-force of only 8 which sold only tables and chairs hand-crafted in shop. The Oakwood-Paradise Company specialized in a style of crafting that was unique to Pondicherry. Business in the early days was excellent with many people around the world wanting to buy the unique style of furniture hand-crafted by the shop. It also attracted the eyes of the Administration of the East India Company and exported many pieces of furniture back to England. These pieces found their home in Palaces and Parliaments around the world.

Mordern Era

The Oakwood Paradise Company has transformed into a multi-storey furniture complex. It crafts all kinds of furniture suitable for any locaiton. Most of the furniture pieces are done using machines. However a custom designs are also done based on customer preferences. Different kinds of services are also available.
